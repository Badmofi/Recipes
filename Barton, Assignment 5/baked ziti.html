Baked Ziti

Servings: 6 to 8
Calories: 535
Prep Time: 75 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ziti pasta
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground meat (your choice)
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 4 cups jarred or homemade pasta sauce
  • 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 heaping cup ricotta cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Add the ziti pasta and cook until al dente.
    Drain and toss with extra virgin olive oil.
  3. In a large skillet over medium high heat, heat a tablespoon of olive oil. Add ground meat when it gets hot enough.
    Brown well until no longer pink.
  4. After the meat has browned, stir in your chopped onion and continue cooking until onions are translucent.
    Add the pasta sauce and stir well. Let it simmer for a while and add seasonings as you like to taste.
  5. Spread a small amount of sauce on the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Dot the surface with half the ricotta cheese.
    Add the pasta to the baking dish, followed by the rest of the sauce, the rest of the ricotta cheese, and sprinkle the mozzarella on top.
  6. Bake in the oven until the top is lightly browned and the cheese has melted nicely for around 20-25 minutes. Let stand for ten minutes before serving.
